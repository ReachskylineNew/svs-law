About the Department

Shaping Legal Minds,
Empowering Women

The idea of a Women's Law College is in tune with the vision of SCSVMV to provide a holistic, value-based, protective educational environment to women, thereby promoting economic and social development of India.

The National Education Policy 2020 aims to empower Women through education and achieve GER 50% for higher education of girls by 2035. Anvikshiki – Women's Law College is committed to this national mission.

🎯 Improved Educational Facility for Rural Women
💪 Recognizing and Enabling Empowerment of Women
📚 Removal of Gender Gaps in Literacy Rates
🏛️ Improvement of Women Leadership Capacity
⚖️ Addressing Gender Equity in Recruitment Processes
🤝 Reducing Gender Stereotyping and Discrimination

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in legal education that empowers women with knowledge, skills, and ethical values to serve society and uphold justice at all levels.

Our Mission

To impart quality legal education through innovative pedagogy, experiential learning, and industry exposure — nurturing women to become competent, compassionate, and courageous legal professionals.

Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure designed to shape future legal professionals

Moot Court

A fully equipped moot court room that simulates a real courtroom experience. Students practice advocacy, argumentation, and courtroom procedures in a professional setting, bridging the gap between academic learning and legal practice.

Learn More →

Moot Court

Law Library

An extensive collection of legal texts, journals, case law reports, and digital resources. The library provides access to major legal databases and online repositories, supporting research, study, and academic excellence for all students.

Learn More →

Law Library

Computer Lab

A dedicated computer lab with modern workstations, providing access to legal research software, online databases, e-resources, and digital learning tools. Students gain essential digital literacy skills vital for modern legal practice.

Learn More →

Computer Lab

Gymnasium

A well-equipped gymnasium providing students with the opportunity to maintain physical fitness and overall well-being. Regular physical activity is encouraged as part of the holistic development approach at SCSVMV.

Learn More →

Gymnasium

Sports Facilities

Comprehensive sports facilities promoting teamwork, discipline, and physical health. Students participate in various indoor and outdoor sports activities, developing competitive spirit and leadership alongside their legal studies.

Learn More →

Sports Facilities

Wellness Center

A dedicated wellness and medical centre ensuring the health and well-being of all students. Provides medical assistance, counselling services, and mental health support to create a nurturing and supportive campus environment.
